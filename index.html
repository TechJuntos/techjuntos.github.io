Reach your full potential in a STEM field with TechJuntos!

TechJuntos is a community of technology enthusiasts from historically under-represented backgrounds who believe that our unique perspectives hold tremendous value to the areas of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We organize workshops, hackathons, speaker seminars, and social events to better prepare students for their transitions to either industry or further academia. We also partner with companies and universities to help them recruit top talent from within our diverse community of motivated students. We'd love for you to join us!


Mentor Program

You will be matched and paired up with an experienced mentor who is invested in your success and will help guide you on your path to becoming a leader in STEM. Our mentors come from a wide range of diverse backgrounds, and are eager to meet you!

Professional Networking

Thanks to our close-knit community, you will be able to meet professionals in both industry and academia who are actively seeking for our members to join their ranks as research assistants, interns, and full time employees. Our regularly hosted meetup events take the stress out of networking!

Industry and Academic Preparation

Whether you're building a working prototype at our hackathons, practicing public speaking at our professional workshops, or gaining inspiration from one of our guests in our speaker seminars, you will gain valuable skills to succeed in the STEM field.

Who makes up the TechJuntos community?

Students

Students at any level are welcome to join our community. Working on your PhD thesis? Preparing for the high school homecoming? TechJuntos has plenty of growth opportunities to help you get to the next step in your professional journey!

Industry

Do you represent a STEM organization who is seeking to expand your innovation by hiring motivated, talented, and curious students who reflect the diversity of your target customers? Partner with TechJuntos and let us help you staff your dream team!

Academia

Research is most effective when the right questions are asked, and the right questions are asked by teams with diverse viewpoints. Our students come from vastly varying backgrounds and experience the world in unique ways. Meet the talented minds who will push your research to the next level.
